Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our club and registration process.

All our registrations are managed through the PlayHQ platform. It's the official platform for community cricket in Australia. Here’s how to get started:

  1. Visit PlayHQ: Click on the "Register Now on PlayHQ" button on our website or go directly to our PlayHQ registration page.
  2. Create an Account: If you don't have a PlayHQ account, you'll need to create one. You’ll also need a Cricket ID. If you don’t have one, you can create one during the process.
  3. Select the Program: Choose the appropriate age group and program for your child. Details about our programs are available on the Programs page.
  4. Complete the Form: Fill in the required details for the player. You can register multiple children under a single parent account.
  5. Make Payment: Follow the prompts to complete the payment securely online.

If you have any trouble, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at yerrabidcc@gmail.com.

We offer a range of programs for different age groups and skill levels, including Woolworths Cricket Blast (for ages 5-10) and various junior competition teams. You can find detailed information about each program on our Programs page.

Our club relies on volunteers to help with coaching, managing teams, scoring, and organizing events. If you're interested in volunteering, please send an email to us at yerrabidcc@gmail.com. We'd love to have you on board!
