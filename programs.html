Find Your Place at Yerrabi

Whether you're picking up a bat for the first time or ready to take your game to the next level, there's a spot for you in the YDCC family. We're committed to providing a fun, safe, and supportive environment for all our players. Explore our programs below to start your cricket journey.

First Innings: Woolworths Cricket Blast (Ages 5-10)

Ready to bat, bowl, and have a ball? Cricket Blast is the perfect introduction to cricket for kids of all abilities. It’s all about fun, friends, and learning the basics through action-packed games. No pads, no heavy bats, just 60-90 minutes of pure enjoyment each week.

  • Focus: Fun, fundamental skills, and teamwork.
  • Commitment: 60-90 minute sessions weekly. All equipment provided!
  • Perfect For: Newcomers to cricket looking for a fun, social, and safe start.

Next Level: Junior Club Cricket (Ages 9-17)

Ready to join a team and play in organised competitions? Our Junior Club teams are the next step. Following Cricket Australia’s official Junior Formats, players join a team and play weekly T20 or One-Day games against other clubs in the region. It's a fantastic way to develop skills, build friendships, and experience the thrill of club cricket.

  • Focus: Skill development, sportsmanship, and structured competition.
  • Commitment: Weekly games and a team training session.
  • Perfect For: Kids ready for team-based competition and skill advancement.
