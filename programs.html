Find Your Place at Yerrabi

Whether you're picking up a bat for the first time or ready to take your game to the next level, there's a spot for you in the YDCC family. We're committed to providing a fun, safe, and supportive environment for all our players. Explore our programs below to start your cricket journey.

Woolworths Cricket Blast

Welcome to the wonderful world of Woolworths Cricket Blast! It’s the ultimate playground for kids of all abilities where the fun never stops and the action is non-stop. Whether you’re a first-time batter or backyard cricket superstar, get ready to embark on an exciting journey filled with laughter, growth, and unforgettable memories. Experience the joy of backyard cricket in a more structured way. Cricket-based game-based activities recreate the magic and lasting memories of pick-up games of cricket.

Pop-up Blast Programs

Come and try your hand at cricket with our accelerated 4-week introductory program – offering the best of Woolworths Cricket Blast, come and experience all the fun and excitement.

Junior Club Cricket

As you grow up, cricket grows with you and it’s fun, fast and action packed! It’s time to join a team, make new friends and take your batting, bowling and fielding skills to the next level. Junior cricket is the next step for kids who are ready to advance their cricket skills through organised games at your local club. The junior cricket pathway nurtures their passion, builds their confidence, allowing them to create lifelong memories.

How to Join Our Club

For New Players

  1. Create a PlayHQ Account: Visit the PlayHQ website and create an account for yourself or your child.
  2. Find Our Club: Search for "Yerrabi District Cricket Club" on the PlayHQ platform.
  3. Choose a Program: Select the program that best fits your age and skill level (e.g., Woolworths Cricket Blast, Junior Cricket).
  4. Complete Registration: Fill out the registration form with all the required details and complete the payment process securely online.
  5. Get Ready to Play: Once registered, you'll receive a confirmation and information about team placements, training schedules, and season start dates.

For Existing Players

  1. Log In to PlayHQ: Visit the PlayHQ website and log in to your existing account.
  2. Re-Register: You should see a prompt to re-register for the new season. If not, navigate to our club page and select the appropriate program.
  3. Confirm Details: Review and update your contact information and any other details to ensure they are current.
  4. Complete Payment: Finalise your registration by completing the online payment.
  5. Welcome Back: You're all set for another great season with YDCC! Keep an eye out for communications from your coach and the club.

Ready to Join the Juniors?
